My name is Jozsef Törjek am in Hungary (Dunube "Holy Benedik")

were born and have lived in Berlin since 1977.

Since my childhood I am working with drawing and paintig.

 

Joszef Törjek its own efforts - from the effects of an inward movement

I have made acquaintance with the painting. My knowledige

I habe learned from books and from imitating the activities of

famous painters.

 

But the lessons of my former teacher, Miklos Varga, who was a very

good painter, has helped me a lot. He showed me in painting beauty to

see, to learn the process and solve various problems.

He was not only a great pinter, but had excellent knowledge of

materials. Some years I have worked with him at my old school.

 

I am lucky, for he has his experience and knowledge shared

with me. Through him I learned the color processing of

powders and Malperspektiven and the paintbrush handle.

 

This was then the solid core of my painting.

I have in my childhood participated in several children´s painting

competitions and won several prizes and awards, which makes me

very proud.

 

I´m with my 23rd Age from other Interessensneigungen ceased to paint.

Later I different courdes of Fine Arts and attended there I got to know many

different artists and learn from them.

 

As a hobby I paint in three years intensive and would now like to show

others my painting and present in thes way my different pictures.

Is is useful to illustrate various themes, so I can find my

last three years of intensive painting in public.

I should be grateful for positive and negative reviews and I hope you like

my pictures and I think in this way in my way of painting.

 

Joszef Törjek
